Snake Brick Run

Drag a bright little snake upward, collect glowing energy, and smash through numbered bricks before your length runs out.

  • Offline Play
  • One-Finger Controls
  • 50 Levels
  • No Login

Snake Brick Run is built for quick arcade sessions: read the board, choose a route, grab power-ups, and keep the snake alive long enough to reach the finish line.

Arcade Challenge, Simple Controls

Grow Fast Collect energy orbs to increase your snake length and prepare for the next wall of bricks.
Break Smart Every brick costs length. Pick the safest path and save enough strength for the finish line.
Use Power-Ups Grab shields, magnets, and growth boosts to survive tougher levels and chase higher scores.
