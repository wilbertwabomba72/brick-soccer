Description

Brick Soccer is a bright arcade brick-bounce game built around quick shots, satisfying ricochets, and graffiti-style visual energy.

Aim the paddle, keep the ball alive, and break through handmade brick patterns across 50 compact stages. Power-ups can split your ball into a wild swarm, add extra chances, or turn a careful shot into a screen-clearing combo. Each level is short enough for a quick break, but the best clears reward timing, positioning, and a little chaos.

  • Fast brick-bounce arcade action
  • 50 unlockable levels
  • Split-ball power-ups and combo-heavy clears
  • Bold graffiti art, punchy effects, and responsive haptics
  • Local progress, best scores, and best times
  • Playable offline with no account required
