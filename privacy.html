Brick Soccer Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Overview

Brick Soccer is a casual arcade game. We designed it to work without an account, without user tracking, and without collecting personal data.

Data We Collect

Brick Soccer does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal data. The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, contacts, photos, advertising identifiers, or payment information.

Local Game Data

Brick Soccer stores gameplay progress locally on your device, including unlocked levels, best scores, best times, star progress, and audio settings. This information is used only to run the game experience on your device. It is not transmitted to us.

Tracking and Analytics

Brick Soccer does not track users across apps or websites. Brick Soccer does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, behavioral profiling, or tracking domains.

Permissions

Brick Soccer does not request access to the camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, calendars, Bluetooth, or health data. The game may use standard device audio and optional haptic feedback to support gameplay.

Children's Privacy

Brick Soccer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not collect personal data, there is no child personal data to request, review, or delete from our servers.

Data Security

Because Brick Soccer does not collect personal data or operate a user account service, we do not maintain personal data on our servers. Local game progress remains on your device and can be removed by deleting the app or clearing the device backup that contains the app data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. If a future version collects data or adds third-party services, this policy will be updated to describe those practices before or when the updated version is released.
