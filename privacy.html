Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Snake Brick Run handles information. Snake Brick Run is an offline arcade game designed to be played without an account, network services, advertising, analytics, or in-app purchases.

Information We Collect

Snake Brick Run does not collect personal information. The game does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or any account credentials.

Local Game Data

The game may save progress locally on your device, such as unlocked levels, best scores, star ratings, and settings for music, sound, and haptics. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Network Access

Snake Brick Run is designed for offline play. The app does not require an internet connection and does not use remote servers for gameplay.

Analytics and Advertising

Snake Brick Run does not include third-party analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking tools, or social login services.

Children's Privacy

The game is suitable for general audiences. Because Snake Brick Run does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information because the app does not collect personal information.

Data Retention

Local progress remains on your device until you delete the app or reset progress inside the game settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app changes. If a future version introduces new data practices, this page will be updated before those changes are released.

Contact

For privacy questions about Snake Brick Run, contact the app publisher through the support information provided with the App Store listing.
